Investment Pool

Visual breakdown of how shareholder investments and bank operating funds combine to reach the maximum investment target

ⓘ
How the Investment Pool Works
The bank sets a maximum investment target for each cycle. The bank covers the full amount from its operating funds if there are not enough shareholders. As shareholders purchase shares, the bank's contribution reduces proportionally, so the total always equals the maximum investment target.

Share Investment Terms
Each share costs 25,000 FCFA. You may hold a maximum of 10 shares. Dividends are distributed after a full one-year cycle. Shares are non-transferable during an active cycle. The bank covers the investment shortfall from its operating funds until enough shareholders participate.

Dividends

Branch-level dividend operations for completed one-year investment cycles

⏰
Dividend Distribution Policy
Dividends are only shared after the completion of a full one-year investment cycle. The dividend rate is determined by the bank's board based on the annual profit and the total investment pool performance. Shareholders must hold shares for the entire cycle to qualify for the dividend payout.
